What is Covid-19?

Covid-19 is a disease caused by a new strain of coronavirus. 'CO' stands for corona, 'VI' for the virus, and 'D' for the disease. It is an infectious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2).
