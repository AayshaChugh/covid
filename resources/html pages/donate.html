Help The Needy Ones!

How about not having a netflix account for a month or spending less of the electricity or less on your expenses? These might be some of the things you can save money from and actually let someone have food for days and weeks.

People at this time are dying not only due to this disease but also because of the lockdown that has caused their savings to finish and these people are left with no money and if they get sick at this moment then they won't be able to survive. But people like us who have a little more than we need can help.

For the country, we should, at this critical situation, learn to mininmize some of our unnecessary expenses and try to help those who are in dire need of their basic amaenities. If we would not watch netflix for a month or would not eat our favourite dishes like pizza etc for a month then this thing might not affect us much but can be a situation changer or life saver for others

So, Below we provide some of the categories which are needed by the people out there. You can donate according to your preference. Select the category you want to donate to or click on the icon buttons on the left sidebar.

Donations for Food

Iskon Dwarka Donations

Iskon Delhi is feeding 5 lacs people daily and now it's your turn to join them. Nearly 2.5 lac daily meals has been sponsored by their gracious donors like you , who have enabled them to stopping this pandemic from turning into a food security crisis.

Hemkunt Foundation

Hemkunt Foundation, a non-profit philanthropic organization based on the most popular Sikh belief ‘Sarbat da bhalla: Blessings for all’. In their journey of over 20 years, their work displayed innovation and irreversible and deep transformations, touching lives of many.

Donations for Helping Financially

Give India

They have been supporting their NGO partners providing Covid-relief and trying to ease the suffering of families caught in the eye of the pandemic storm – with donations from you. They have also received support from corporates, HNIs and philanthropic foundations and many donations through Facebook.

PM Cares Fund

Keeping in mind the need for having a dedicated national fund with the primary objective of dealing with any kind of emergency or distress situation, like posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, and to provide relief to the affected, a public charitable trust, ‘Prime Minister’s Citizen Assistance and Relief in Emergency Situations Fund’ has been set up.

Donations for Clothes

Clothes Box Foundation

Clothes Box Foundation Pickups the clothes you want to donate from your doorstep. Once you have kept aside the clothes you wish you donate, you can call CBF or visit their Facebook page and leave them a message.

Clothes For Help

When you donate to them, they sort and deliver these much needed items directly to families living in serious poverty. They visit different slums across the city to make sure your clothes reach the people who need them.

Donations for Medicine

Medicine Baba

They are serving since 10 years to Provide free medicines to the one in need. They are happy to dedicate themselves in service of the poor.

Uday Foundation

With your kind generosity, we are able to treat thousands of underprivileged children from basic illness to severe medical condition with special emphasis on vitamin deficiencies and malnutrition that effects of every illness.

Donations for Education

Action Aid

When you sponsor a child with ActionAid, you not only help the child in need but also transform the lives of children in the community where the sponsored child lives, by ensuring they get access to quality education, proper nutrition, healthcare and protection.

AkshayPatra

The Akshaya Patra Foundation is an NGO in India headquartered in Bengaluru. Their organisation strives to eliminate classroom hunger by implementing the Mid-Day Meal Scheme in the government schools and government-aided schools.
